About

Resume, education, skills and goals!

Ali Berdan Karasoy

Hello! I am a software developer born in Istanbul in 1999 and graduated from Duzce University with a degree in Computer Engineering. I have been working in mobile programming and game development for about three years, and I have also focused on web design and programming for the past two years.

I have experience developing various applications on mobile platforms using Java, Kotlin, Swift, and Dart/Flutter. Additionally, I create games on Unity using C#, and I develop backend and data analysis solutions with Python. On the web side, I design and develop modern and user-friendly websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

With my English proficiency at B1 level, I am able to effectively work on international projects. Thanks to my passion for technology and hunger for continuous learning, I continually improve myself and adapt to new technologies.

Producing creative solutions, prioritizing user experience, and integrating technology into people's daily lives are just a few of my important goals. If you are looking for a software developer who will add value to your project, feel free to contact me!
